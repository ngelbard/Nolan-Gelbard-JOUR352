10.  Why do we use media queries?

Media queries allow for us to define different style rules for different devices. It targets different width and heights, or different orientations, to tailor the style sheet accordingly.


11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

Serif fonts contain small strokes at the edges of each letter, while sans-serif fonts have clean lines. Serif is more elegant, sans-serif is more minimalist.
